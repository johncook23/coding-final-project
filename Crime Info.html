    Crime in Chicago

    Crime is very prevelant in Chicago (as seen in the figure below). It is important to feel safe in your new home. We want you to find a neighborhood where you feel secure and comfortable.

    Chicago Crime Map

    Below, we have provide a detailed map showing crime incidents across Chicago neighborhoods. The darker shades of purple indicate lower crime rates, while the more yellow shades indicate higher crime rates. Using this map, you can identify safer areas to consider for your new home!

    As expected, this map tells us that locations closer to inner city are generally associated with higher crime rates, while neighborhoods further from the center tend to be safer. In addition, the southern parts of the city tend to have higher crime rates compared to the northern parts. While traffic commutes are a concern for many people, maybe consider living a bit further outside of the city center to ensure your safety.
